Drone Project

Project Overview

I have contributed to a team project involving the development of a specialized drone for agricultural purposes. This drone, equipped with cutting-edge sensors and imaging technology, is designed to monitor crop health, soil conditions, and irrigation requirements. Our goal is to further refine and modify this drone to enhance its utility in agriculture, ultimately enabling farmers to optimize their crop management practices and increase yields. This project highlights my ability to collaborate on innovative solutions and my commitment to leveraging technology for agricultural advancements.
