Project 2

Gas Leakage Detector Project

Project Overview

Description: I collaborated with a team to develop a gas leakage detector using Arduino. The project aimed to detect the presence of gases such as methane, propane, or carbon monoxide in the environment, ensuring safety in homes and industries. We used an Arduino board, a gas sensor module (MQ-2), a buzzer, and an LED for visual indication. Responsibilities: Assisted in the circuit design and component selection Contributed to coding the Arduino to read sensor values and activate the alarm and LED Participated in testing and calibration of the sensor for accurate gas detection Achievements: Successfully developed a functional gas leakage detector prototype Demonstrated the project to our peers and received positive feedback Improved my skills in Arduino programming and sensor interfacing This project enhanced my understanding of electronics and programming, and I gained valuable experience in teamwork and project management.
